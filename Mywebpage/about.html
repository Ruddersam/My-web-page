Hello, World! (About Me)

Hi, I'm Samuel, a Ghana-based computer science student at KNUST with a passion for solving real-world problems through technology. I enjoy building modern, user-friendly mobile and web applications—most of my recent work blends React Native on the frontend with Spring Boot on the backend.

I'm always curious and love exploring new ideas, whether it's designing elegant UI screens, experimenting with authentication flows, or analyzing how systems work. My projects often begin as simple ideas and grow into full platforms—like apps that connect workers to contractors or tools that help users manage daily tasks.

Beyond code, I have a deep love for aviation. I dream of building an Aviation Gallery that hosts aircraft exhibitions, air shows, and flight-school experiences. I've also been interested in creating a spotting community here in Ghana, connecting people who share the passion of watching and photographing aircraft.

I value teamwork and leadership; I've previously served as a Public Relations Officer and have worked with teams of developers on real application concepts, balancing UI design, backend logic, and documentation.

Every day, I strive to learn, create, and share—to turn imagination into working solutions.
